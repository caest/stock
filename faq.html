What is the minimum order quantity?

The minimum order starts from 100 kg (by weight) or from 1 pallet. This allows even small businesses to start with flexible quantities.

Can I order by weight or only by pallet?

Yes, we offer both options. You can order mixed stock by weight or full pallets depending on your needs.

What types of products do you offer?

We offer branded stock clothing, footwear, and accessories for women, men, and children. Goods are available in different segments (A, B, C) depending on condition and assortment.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our sales manager via email or phone. After confirmation, we prepare your invoice and shipment details.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Orders can be modified before dispatch. Once goods are shipped, changes are no longer possible.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we deliver to most EU countries and beyond. Shipping costs depend on the destination and weight of the order.
